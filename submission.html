Application Submission

We are excited to invite you to present your geospatial software application!

Following this year's twist of “Geospatial Technologies: Fancy toys or tangible outcomes” we are giving students, professionals and researchers the opportunity to present their geospatial applications. Through this submission we would like to promote and encourage practical solutions to geospatial problems, as opposed to purely theoretical projects.

So if you have developed software or application, regardless of size and complexity, using geospatial data we give you a platform to demonstrate and promote this to the geospatial community.

Applying is easy, just fill up this form.

Submit

We will get back to our selected developers by an email and inform them about further presentation details. The official language of the conference is English.

For any questions about templates and formats please send us an email to organization[at]geomundus.org

The submission deadline is October 30, 2021.
